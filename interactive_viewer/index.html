Digital PCR Endpoint Quantification

Endpoint dPCR classifies each partition as positive or negative after amplification. The positive fraction is converted to the mean template occupancy per partition using Poisson statistics.

p = k / n λ = -ln(1 - p) copies = λ × n
  • k: number of positive partitions.
  • n: number of accepted partitions after QC filtering.
  • p: positive-partition fraction.
  • λ: mean target copies per partition after Poisson correction.
